Dr. Adam Watchm

As a Medical Scribe at Aim Medical Center (June 2023 – Present, Georgia), I record detailed History of Present Illness (HPIs) in compliance with HIPAA regulations while utilizing medical software for efficient patient documentation. My role has strengthened my proficiency in medical terminology, enhanced my typing speed and accuracy, and deepened my understanding of HIPAA compliance protocols.

Previously, I completed a one-month Clinical Rotation in the ENT Surgery Ward at Aga Khan University Hospital (July 2022, Karachi) under the mentorship of a leading ENT surgeon. This experience refined my professional conduct, empathy, and interpersonal skills while adhering to the hospital’s strict hygiene and cleanliness standards.

Through these roles, I have gained hands-on clinical exposure, developed strong documentation skills, and cultivated a patient-centered approach to healthcare delivery.
